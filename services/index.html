Three scenarios. One team.

How real engagements unfold across the network when the situation calls for more than one discipline.

Scenario 01
The tax bomb.

A holder needs significant liquidity, but selling triggers a tax event larger than they want to realize.

01
Basilic
Frames the goal against the constraint and convenes the team. Identifies which paths preserve the position and which require disposition.
02
Falcon Rappaport & Berkman
Structures the legal mechanism — a Bitcoin-collateralized facility, a charitable vehicle, or an entity restructure — that delivers liquidity without a forced sale.
03
Satoshi Pacioli
Models the after-tax outcome of each option side-by-side, with the year's overall tax position factored in.
04
Bitcoin Well
Stands ready if any on-chain movement is required to complete the structure.

The client gets the liquidity. The position stays intact.

Scenario 02
The high-income year.

A founder exit, a windfall, or an unusually large income year — and a window to convert dollars into Bitcoin with real tax efficiency.

01
Satoshi Pacioli
Quantifies the year's tax exposure and identifies which strategies — mining depreciation, charitable vehicles, retirement contributions — make sense at this scale.
02
Abundant Mines
Stands up hosted mining where depreciation is the right lever, with custody and operations turnkey from day one.
03
Bitcoin Well
Executes OTC for the cash position, with Bitcoin delivered directly to the client's custody.
04
Basilic
Anchors the deployed capital inside the broader plan and sets the cadence for the years ahead.

The income year becomes an accumulation year — on the client's terms.

Scenario 03
The succession problem.

A spouse without access protocols. Children not read in. An estate document that treats Bitcoin like a brokerage account.

01
Falcon Rappaport & Berkman
Drafts the legal architecture for Bitcoin inheritance — multi-signature access, key agents, and trust structures purpose-built for self-custody.
02
Basilic
Maps the generational financial plan and translates the legal structure into a living roadmap the family can actually follow.
03
Satoshi Pacioli
Models the tax mechanics of lifetime gifting, transfers, and the eventual estate event so each decision is made with the full picture.

The wealth survives the holder. The family knows what to do.
