What We Do

Bitcoin Family Office Services

The platform coordinates services for a small number of families holding significant Bitcoin positions. The work is structural — custody architecture, financial planning, wealth management, tax optimization, acquisition & utilization strategies, asset protection, governance, and estate planning.

The advisory model for traditional family offices was built for equities, real estate, and fixed income. Bitcoin does not behave like any of these assets. It is a bearer instrument. It has no counterparty. It cannot be custodied by a bank. It can be inherited, but only if the technical and legal infrastructure is deliberately constructed.

This requires a different kind of advisor — one who understands both the monetary architecture of Bitcoin and the legal structures available to preserve wealth across time. The platform and its service providers are built around that intersection.

Custody Architecture

Bitcoin Well · Basilic Financial · Abundant Mines

Custody is not a technical detail — it is the foundational decision from which everything else follows. A poorly designed custody architecture is an existential risk. A well-designed one provides sovereignty, resilience, and succession continuity across generations. Through a network of specialized providers, the platform coordinates custody frameworks, hardware selection, geographic distribution strategies, and operational security protocols for families holding significant Bitcoin positions.

  • Quorum design (2-of-3, 3-of-5, and custom configurations)
  • Hardware wallet selection and hardening
  • Geographic key distribution frameworks
  • Operational security and access control protocols
  • Custody provider evaluation and due diligence
  • Inheritance-ready custody architecture
02

Estate Planning & Succession

Falcon, Rapport, and Berkman LLP

Traditional estate planning assumes custodial assets held by institutions. Bitcoin breaks that model entirely. The mechanisms of control — cryptographic keys, seed phrases, quorums — require specialized expertise. Through independent legal professionals, succession frameworks are designed that respect the bearer nature of Bitcoin while ensuring orderly transfer of control across generations.

  • Bitcoin inheritance protocol design
  • Trust structures for Bitcoin holdings
  • Signing frameworks and executor protocols
  • Multi-generational governance structure
  • Coordination with executors and trustees
  • Heir education and onboarding frameworks
03

Tax Strategy & Structuring

Satoshi Pacioli · Bitment

Tax planning and filing services, coordinated through independent tax professionals, are a crucial layer across a client's overall strategy. The tax treatment of Bitcoin is complex, evolving, and jurisdiction-dependent. Significant positions require thoughtful structuring — not to evade obligation, but to deploy legal mechanisms that align the timing of recognition events with family priorities.

  • Tax optimization and filing
  • Cost basis management and lot-selection strategy
  • Annual gifting program design
  • Entity structure selection (LLC, trust, family limited partnership)
  • Jurisdiction analysis for estate and income tax optimization
