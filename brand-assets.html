Usage Notes

  • Minimum size: 13px — never render the lockup smaller than this
  • Clear space: maintain at least 1× the height of the ₿ character on all sides
  • The ₿ symbol uses gold (#c9a84c) on dark backgrounds, and near-black (#1a1a1a) on light
  • No logomark exists beyond the ₿ character — do not create or substitute one
  • Never stretch, rotate, or apply effects to the logo text

04 — Voice & Tone

How We Sound

First-principles Bitcoin thinking. No hype, no speculation. We speak to people who already understand the thesis.

First Principles

Every claim traces back to Bitcoin's fundamental properties: fixed supply, decentralization, censorship resistance, bearer asset.

No Hype

Zero speculation language. No price predictions. No urgency manipulation. No "crypto-bro" energy.

Peer-to-Peer

Speaks peer-to-peer, not salesperson-to-prospect. Our audience holds $5M+ and already owns Bitcoin.

Authoritative, Not Condescending

Technically precise and calm. Professional but never corporate. Confident but never arrogant.

Tone References

Jeff Booth · Lynn Alden · Preston Pysh · Parker Lewis — this is the register we inhabit.

Audience

$5M+ net worth Bitcoin holders. They don't need convincing — they need structuring, preservation, and multi-generational strategy.

Examples

✓ Do

"Bitcoin's fixed supply creates unique estate planning challenges that traditional advisors aren't equipped to handle."
"A properly structured dynasty trust can eliminate estate tax across multiple generations."

✗ Don't

"Bitcoin is going to the moon! 🚀 Don't miss out on this amazing opportunity!"
"We guarantee tax savings of millions of dollars!"

Photography Style

Dark, moody, professional. Think dimly lit offices, architectural details, and abstract textures. No stock photos of smiling people in suits. No handshakes. No generic financial imagery.

Icon Style

Minimal line icons — 1.5px stroke, consistent sizing. Prefer geometric simplicity. No filled/solid icons. No emoji in UI contexts.
