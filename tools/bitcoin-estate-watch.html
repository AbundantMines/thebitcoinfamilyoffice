Why This Exists

Most Bitcoin Holders Are Flying Blind on Estate Taxes

Bitcoin's price moves daily. Your estate tax exposure moves with it. A $5M position today could create $2M in estate tax liability by the time your heirs inherit it — and that number changes every time the price moves.

Most Bitcoin holders check their portfolio every day. Almost none are watching what that means for their estate tax exposure. Bitcoin Estate Watch does it for you.

How It Works

Three Steps. Continuous Awareness.

01
Enter your holdings once

Provide your BTC amount, total other assets, and state. That's all we need.

02
We monitor daily

Every day, we fetch the current BTC price, recalculate your federal estate tax exposure, and compare it against your exemption threshold.

03
You get an alert

The moment your exposure crosses a threshold that triggers a planning action, you get an email — not after the fact, not quarterly, the day it happens.
