## Brand Voice

- **Tone:** First principles Bitcoin thinking. No hype, no speculation language.
- **Influences:** Jeff Booth, Lynn Alden, Preston Pysh
- **Audience:** $5M+ net worth holders who already own Bitcoin
- **Register:** Authoritative, calm, technically precise. Speaks peer-to-peer, not salesperson-to-prospect.
- **Avoid:** "To the moon", price predictions, urgency manipulation, crypto-bro language, speculation framing

## Logo

- Currently text-based: **₿** + "The Bitcoin Family Office" in Playfair Display
- Gold (₿ symbol) on dark background, or dark on white for print
- No logomark beyond the ₿ character
