Several proven strategies can minimize or eliminate federal and state estate taxes on Bitcoin: (1) GRATs allow you to transfer appreciated Bitcoin out of your estate, capturing future appreciation tax-free; (2) Dynasty Trusts in states like Wyoming can hold Bitcoin for multiple generations outside the estate tax system; (3) The Buy-Borrow-Die strategy enables liquidity without a taxable event; (4) SLATs can remove assets while retaining indirect access. The right combination depends on your estate size, domicile state, and planning horizon.
